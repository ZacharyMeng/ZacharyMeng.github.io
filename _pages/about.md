# About me
ZacharyMeng is a second-year Ph.D. candidate (2023-) at
[SMULL Group](https://cszhengzhang.cn/SMULL/) of [HITsz CS](http://cs.hitsz.edu.cn) advised by Prof. [Zheng Zhang](https://cszhengzhang.cn/).
